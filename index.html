The largest programming community at Richmond Secondary School.

The RHS Programming club is a place for like-minded programming enthusiasts or people interested in programming to gather in the school community. RHS has always lacked a good community for programmers, whether for formal classes, or extracurricular activities. As a result, the programming club was formed to fill this gap and provide an opportunity for programmers in the school community to learn and have fun. Our club welcomes students of all levels as long as they are willing to learn and are passionate about programming.
